About us


La Berceuse d'or Residence is a private residence for senior citizens located by the water which offers a splendid view. We offer services for autonomous and semi-autonomous elderly, retired citizens, short-term and recovering residents as well as people with Alzheimer's or memory loss. Our 14 rooms include a sink to ensure the comfort of our senior citizens. Our residence offers a safe and secure space for our residents with a 24/7 surveillance with indoor and outdoor cameras. We also offer medication management to ensure our residents' peace of mind. Our personnel speaks English, French, Arabic, Chinese and Creole.
